# Logix Contact Overview

## Who Logix Contact Is

Logix Contact is a UK web and app development agency that helps businesses design, build, and grow digital products. The team combines strategy, design, and engineering to deliver practical, high-quality software solutions for startups, SMEs, and growing brands.

## What Logix Contact Does

Logix Contact provides end-to-end digital services, including:

- Web Development (custom websites and web applications)
- Mobile App Development (iOS and Android solutions)
- UI/UX Design (user-focused product and interface design)
- Digital Marketing (SEO and growth-oriented digital support)
- Cloud Solutions (deployment, architecture, and optimization)
- IT Consultancy (technical planning and product strategy)

The agency supports projects from discovery and planning through design, development, launch, and continuous improvement.
